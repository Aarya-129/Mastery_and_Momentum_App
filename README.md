<h2>📌 Overview</h2>

<div class="card">
<p>
Mastery & Momentum is built around the philosophy of consistent daily execution.
It combines scheduling, habit tracking, and progress analytics into a clean,
minimal interface that eliminates distractions and maximizes focus.
</p>
</div>

---

<h2>✨ Features</h2>

<div class="card">
<ul>
<li>📅 Structured Daily Schedule (Time-blocking)</li>
<li>✅ Non-negotiable habit tracking</li>
<li>📊 90-day progress tracking system</li>
<li>📝 Notes & reflections system</li>
<li>🔥 Streak tracking</li>
<li>🔔 Smart reminders & notifications</li>
<li>📱 Installable as a mobile app (PWA)</li>
<li>⚡ Works offline using Service Worker</li>
</ul>
</div>

---
